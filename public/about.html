Our Story

Passion, Precision, and Elegance woven into every thread.

Handcrafted with Love

At Mouni Corner, we believe that true beauty lies in the details. What started as a small passion project of wrapping simple bangles in vibrant silk threads quickly blossomed into a fully dedicated handmade jewelry business.

Each piece you see in our store—whether it’s a beautifully wrapped bangle, intricately designed earrings, or an ornate hair accessory—is 100% handcrafted by us. We spend hours sourcing the finest quality silk threads, stones, beads, and raw materials so that the end product isn't just an accessory, but a piece of art.

Our mission is to bring affordable, premium-quality, and highly customizable handmade jewelry that makes you feel confident and glowing.

The Craft

We combine traditional Indian artisanship with modern aesthetic sensibilities. The result? Unique items that look spectacular with ethnic wear and surprisingly chic with western attire.
